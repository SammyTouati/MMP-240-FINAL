Typography Poster Design:



For this Design, the goal was to make a poster to redesign a typography website. For this project, I used Adobe XD, and changed up the typeface from the original one shown on the website. I wanted to make the title of the whole Typography Festival stand out, which is why I used the black and white checker pattern to enhance the appearance of the main title text as well.
